Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Pulse Global operates an automation platform focused on creating, managing and publishing news and informational content across supported social media platforms. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be processed when using our services, how that information is handled and the principles we follow to protect it.

Scope

This policy applies to Pulse Global services, websites, automation workflows, integrations and APIs. Our infrastructure currently relies on technologies including n8n, Airtable, Telegram Bot API, OpenAI API, Cloudinary, GitHub Pages, Facebook Graph API and, where applicable, Instagram Graph API and Google Drive.

Information We Process

  • Technical identifiers required to execute authorized publishing requests.
  • Content intentionally submitted for publication.
  • Scheduling metadata, workflow status and operational logs.
  • API access tokens only where explicitly configured by the owner of the automation.

Information We Do Not Intentionally Collect

  • We do not build consumer profiles.
  • We do not sell personal information.
  • We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information unless required by a connected platform.

Purpose of Processing

Information is processed solely to automate publication, manage editorial workflows, generate AI-assisted content, maintain service integrity and improve operational reliability.

Commercial Activity

Pulse Global is intended to operate as a commercial media project. Revenue may be generated through advertising, sponsored content, commercial partnerships and other lawful media activities. Advertising relationships do not alter this privacy policy.

Third-Party Services

Connected services maintain their own privacy policies. Pulse Global may exchange only the minimum information necessary to execute requested operations with Facebook, Instagram, Telegram, Airtable, OpenAI, Cloudinary and similar providers.

Data Retention

Operational records may be retained only as long as reasonably necessary for workflow execution, troubleshooting, legal compliance and security.

Security

Reasonable technical and organizational safeguards are used. No Internet-based system can guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

Where applicable, you may request access, correction or deletion of information associated with your interactions with Pulse Global.

International Processing

Data may be processed in multiple jurisdictions through trusted infrastructure providers.

Policy Updates

This document may be updated as the platform evolves.
