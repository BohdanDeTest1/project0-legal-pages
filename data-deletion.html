Data Deletion Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Pulse Global respects requests for deletion of information where legally applicable.

Request Procedure

  1. Send a request to derba.se.b@gmail.com.
  2. Identify the connected platform and relevant account.
  3. Describe the information you want removed.

Verification

We may request reasonable verification before processing a deletion request.

Exceptions

Processing Time

Eligible requests are normally processed within a reasonable timeframe after verification.
